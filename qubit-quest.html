// INCOMING TRANSMISSION — PRIORITY ALPHA Engineer. The station's quantum memory core has been scrambled by a rogue AI. Quantum computers store information as qubits — the quantum equivalent of a bit. A rogue AI has used quantum gates to scramble those qubits into the wrong states. Your job is to unscramble them by applying the correct sequence of gates.

Each chamber is locked behind a quantum state — a qubit frozen in the wrong configuration. Apply quantum gates to rotate each qubit on a live 3D Bloch Sphere to match the target state and unlock the chamber.

No quantum physics degree required. The sphere will guide you.
